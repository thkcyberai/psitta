# Psitta 2.0
## Introducing the Writing Nook Platform

Psitta 2.0 is the largest evolution of the product since its first release.

Psitta is now one platform with one purpose: helping writers finish books. Everything you need — planning, drafting, revising, and hearing your work read back — lives in a single, unified Writing Nook experience.

### Highlights

- **One unified Writing Nook platform** — one application, one workspace, one consistent experience from first idea to finished manuscript. No more switching between product modes; everything is a capability of the platform, right where you need it.
- **Simplified subscription model** — one product, one decision. Start free and explore the platform, then unlock everything with Writing Nook: a 14-day free trial, $17.99/month or $183/year. Creative Nook is coming soon.
- **Capability-based architecture** — features unlock instantly and consistently across the entire app the moment you subscribe, with the server as the single source of truth.
- **New Plans experience** — see exactly what you can already do, what's waiting for you, and where you stand: your trial status with days remaining, your active subscription at a glance, and one-click subscription management.
- **Story Development improvements** — plan the book before it drifts, with scene mapping and progress tracking that build as you write.
- **Blueprint system** — living outlines with 25+ proven narrative structures (Three-Act, Save the Cat, Seven-Point, Snowflake, and more), each with an interactive step-by-step guide.
- **Structure Analyzer** — honest, section-by-section feedback on pacing, gaps, and beats, measured against the structure you chose.
- **Story Coach** — an always-on coach that nudges you the moment a scene drifts from your outline, so you course-correct while writing instead of on the rewrite.
- **Writing-focused workflow** — a distraction-free Writing Desk built for daily writing, with your whole manuscript organized in projects and one searchable library.
- **Platform-wide UX consistency** — the website, pricing, and the desktop app now tell one story, in English, Portuguese, Spanish, and French.
- **Improved Stripe subscription management** — manage your plan, payment method, and invoices directly from the app, including during your trial.
- **Hundreds of engineering improvements and fixes** across performance, reliability, entitlements, playback, and localization.

### Reading & Revision — built in

Hearing your work is still the heart of Psitta: natural voices, word- and sentence-level highlighting, and playback up to 4× are all part of Writing Nook. Your ear catches what your eye misses — and in 2.0, that revision loop is woven into the writing workflow itself.

### Availability

Psitta 2.0 is available for Windows 10 and 11 at [psitta.ai/download](https://psitta.ai/download). Existing installations update automatically. Your account, documents, and projects carry over — nothing to migrate, nothing to reconfigure.
